# Jurisdiction Pre-Resolve Map

Before searching, tag the topic with one or more jurisdictions and resolve the right regulators.

| Code | Regulators & courts | Typical business-law topics |
|------|---------------------|----------------------------|
| `EU` | EC, EDPB, ESMA, national DPAs | GDPR, AI Act, CSRD, competition, DMA |
| `UK` | FCA, ICO, Companies House, UK Parliament | Economic Crime Act, UK GDPR, employment |
| `US` | SEC, FTC, OFAC, CFPB, Federal Register | Sanctions, securities, antitrust, privacy |
| `IN` | MCA, RBI, SEBI, MeitY, DGT | DPDP, FDI, FEMA, Companies Act, NI Act |
| `SG` | MAS, ACRA | Fintech licensing, employment, arbitration |
| `AE` | DFSA, ADGM, DIFC | Free-zone corporate, banking, sanctions |
| `MULTI` | WTO, ICC, UNCITRAL | Trade disputes, cross-border M&A, shipping |

## Sector → jurisdiction shortcuts

| Sector | Default jurisdictions |
|--------|----------------------|
| Maritime / shipping | MULTI + US (OFAC) + EU + IN |
| Fintech / payments | SG + EU + IN + US |
| Data / SaaS | EU + UK + IN (DPDP) + US |
| Manufacturing FDI | IN + EU + US |
| HNWI / family office | IN + UK + AE + US |
